### 🛡️ AI WORKING STANDARDS (THE SYSTEM)

- **Do NOT "Simplify"**: Never reduce complex CSS to "cleaner" code if it sacrifices the high-fidelity depth, gradients, or glassmorphism.
- **Maintain "God-Mode" Aesthetic**: Every addition must feel premium (Ocean/Midnight palettes, 30px+ soft shadows, Outfit/JetBrains Mono typography).
- **Layered UI**: Respect the "Depth-Heavy" philosophy. Elements must feel physically layered above the grid.

#### 2. Structural Integrity
- **Tactical Naming**: Use tactical nomenclature (e.g., `ops-card`, `signal-bar`, `telemetry-ticker`) to maintain the battlefield mindset.
- **Component Isolation**: When adding new "Tiles" or "Screens," prioritize standalone readability within their designated folders (e.g., `tile-design-v2/`).

#### 3. Interaction Protocol
- **Micro-Animations**: All interactive elements MUST have transition layers (`0.4s cubic-bezier` or similar).
- **Tactical Feedback**: Actions should trigger visual "HUD" responses—pulses, glow changes, or watermark overlays.
