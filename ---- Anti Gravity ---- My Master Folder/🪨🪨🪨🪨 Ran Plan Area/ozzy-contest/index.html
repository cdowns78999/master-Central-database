"Patient Number 9" Album Campaign

2024 Clio Music Awards — Bronze, Integrated Campaign

Epic Records' campaign for Patient Number 9 — cover art plus Todd McFarlane comic and video integration — officially took Bronze at the 2024 Clio Music Awards in the Integrated Campaign category. The cleanest citable "winner" trail in the entire Ozzy art-contest universe.

2025 Totton, Hampshire · UK

Sunnyfields Farm Pumpkin Mural

Public Vote Winner — 10,000 pumpkins & squash

Sunnyfields Farm ran a public online vote to pick the subject of their 2025 mural — Ozzy won. Sixteen staff built the portrait (round glasses, three bats) in under five hours. Sharon, Jack and Kelly Osbourne visited the farm in person on October 11, 2025.

2025 Birmingham · UK

Mr Murals — Navigation Street Black Sabbath Mural

Commissioned Work · Signed by all four members

A 40-metre commissioned mural outside Birmingham New Street station by Birmingham-born artist Mr Murals (Darren Quinton), signed on June 28 by all four original Sabbath members. Ozzy gifted the artist two tickets to the Back to the Beginning farewell show as thanks — the closest thing to a "prize" in this non-contest commission.

Jon Jones Sabbath Portrait — "Working Class Hero"

Birmingham Museum & Art Gallery · extended through Sept 2026

Castle Fine Art commissioned artist Jon Jones to create a limited-edition Black Sabbath portrait specifically for the Birmingham Museum & Art Gallery's Working Class Hero exhibit — the world-first Ozzy exhibition, extended twice due to public demand.

2007 Online · USA

"I Don't Wanna Stop" Fan Video Contest

Ozzy Official Site + YouTube · green-screen footage

Ozzy released green-screen performance footage and invited fans to cut their own music video. The winning fan-made cut was adopted as an official video; the follow-up Not Going Away contest was launched because Ozzy was so happy with the submissions.
