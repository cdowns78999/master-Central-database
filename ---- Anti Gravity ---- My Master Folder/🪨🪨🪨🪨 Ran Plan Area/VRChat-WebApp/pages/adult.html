18+ Content in VRChat

VRChat's adult community has existed since the platform's early days. This section provides factual, non-sensational information about how adult content is structured, regulated, and safely navigated within the platform. The goal is harm reduction and informed awareness.

Policy Timeline

Sep 2023
Content Gating — VRChat introduced age-based content labels. Worlds can now be tagged as 18+ and hidden from users who haven't verified their age. A major step toward structured content separation.
Jan 2025
Age Verification — VRChat rolled out identity-based age verification. Users must verify to access 18+ labeled worlds. Verification is handled by a third-party provider with privacy protections.
Current
Current Rules — 18+ worlds require verified accounts. Content must stay within labeled spaces. Public instances of non-labeled worlds must remain appropriate for all ages. Enforcement is active.

Community Culture

ERP (Erotic Roleplay) is a longstanding part of VRChat's social ecosystem. It exists primarily in private instances and age-verified worlds. The culture ranges from casual social encounters to established community groups with their own norms, spaces, and safety protocols.

Like all social spaces in VR, the experience is shaped by the people in it. Many participants describe it as an extension of VR's core appeal: presence, embodiment, and genuine human connection in ways that flat-screen platforms cannot replicate.

Consent & Safety

Consent is the foundation of all healthy interactions. VRChat provides several built-in tools to protect personal boundaries, and the community has developed additional norms around communication and respect.

🛡️

Safety Bubble

Personal space shield. Prevents other avatars from entering your physical space. Adjustable radius.

🚫

Block & Mute

Instantly remove anyone from your experience. They cannot see, hear, or interact with you.

🔒

Private Instances

Invite-only rooms. Full control over who enters. The standard for intimate interactions.

🗣️

Communication

Establish boundaries verbally before any interaction. "Ask first" is the community standard.

Haptic Integration

Some users integrate haptic devices (such as Lovense products) with VRChat through OSC protocol. These setups allow avatar interactions to trigger real-world tactile feedback. The technology is user-configured and requires third-party software to bridge VRChat's avatar parameters to Bluetooth-connected devices.
