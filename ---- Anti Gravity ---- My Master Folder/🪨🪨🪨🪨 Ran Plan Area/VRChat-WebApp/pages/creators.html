Creator Spotlight

M

Mochie

Avatar Artist • Shader Developer

Known for the wildly popular Mochie's Avatar Shader suite and custom anime avatars that push the boundaries of VRChat's rendering pipeline. Their work has been used by over 50,000 creators worldwide.

"The VRChat community taught me that creativity isn't just about what you make — it's about who you make it for."

Interview

How did you get started creating for VRChat?

I was a 3D art hobbyist who got pulled into VRChat by friends in 2019. I saw people wearing these incredible custom avatars and thought, "I need to learn how to do that." I started with kitbashing — combining free assets — and eventually moved into making everything from scratch. The feedback loop in VR is unlike anything else. Someone wears your creation and you can literally see them react to it in real time.

What's the hardest part of creating avatars for VRChat?

Optimization. Making something that looks stunning on a high-end PC but also runs on Quest standalone is a massive challenge. You're working with strict polygon budgets, material limits, and texture sizes. It's like making game-ready assets on hard mode. But the constraint is what makes it creative — you have to get inventive with shaders, LODs, and smart topology.

What advice would you give new creators?

Don't be afraid to kitbash at first. Nobody starts from zero. Use free bases, learn the SDK, join creator communities. The VRChat Creator Companion (VCC) makes the pipeline way smoother than it used to be. Also: share your work early. The community is genuinely supportive, and you'll get better faster from real feedback than from tutorial hell.
