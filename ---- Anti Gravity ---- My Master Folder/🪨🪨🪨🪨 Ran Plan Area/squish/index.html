In-Game Avatar
Creation Tools
Blender
Free
Open-source 3D suite — modeling, sculpting, rigging, animation, FBX export. The main tool for custom VRChat avatars from scratch.
Pro Quality Advanced Needs Unity
Unity + VRChat SDK
Required
Every avatar must pass through Unity + VRChat SDK. No exceptions. VRChat Creator Companion handles setup automatically.
Intermediate Unity 2022.3.22f1 SDK 3.10.2
VRoid Studio
Free
Anime-style character creator by Pixiv. Slider-based — no modeling needed. Exports VRM, new XAvatar format for VRChat.
Mid Quality Beginner Anime Only
Tafi
Freemium
400+ customization components. Auto-optimized for PC + Quest. Text-to-3D character engine in beta. DAZ3D subsidiary.
High Quality Beginner VRChat-Ready
Vket Avatar Maker
Free
100% browser-based — no install needed. Create anime avatars and upload directly to VRChat from the browser.
Mid Quality Beginner Direct Upload
Mixamo
Free
Adobe's auto-rigging + animation library. Upload a model, mark 5 landmarks, get a full humanoid rig. Thousands of free animations.
Mid Quality Beginner Rigging Only
DAZ 3D
Freemium
Pro-grade realistic human figures (Genesis 8/9). Massive asset library. Heavy optimization needed for VRChat — these are render models.
High Quality Advanced Needs Optimization
MakeHuman
Free
Open-source parametric human creator. Slider-based body/face. Dated aesthetics but good as a starting base for Blender refinement.
Low/Mid Beginner Base Mesh
MetaHuman
License Blocked
Film-quality digital humans by Epic Games. Highest fidelity available — but license prohibits use outside Unreal Engine. Cannot use in VRChat.
Pro Quality Advanced UE Only
Ready Player Me
Shut Down
Selfie-to-avatar builder. Netflix acquired Dec 2025, SDK shut down Jan 30, 2026. No longer available.
Was Beginner RIP Jan 2026
Adobe Fuse CC
Dead
Adobe's modular character creator. Discontinued Sept 2020. Not recommended for new projects.
Was Free w/ CC RIP 2020
AI-Powered Generation
Tripo AI
Freemium
Text/image to 3D with universal auto-rigging. Clean quad topology. Closest to VRChat-ready of any AI tool — still needs Unity pipeline.
Mid Quality Beginner Nearly Ready
Meshy AI
Freemium
Fast AI 3D generation with Blender/Unity plugins. Reliable, consistent results. Needs rigging + Unity processing.
Mid Quality Beginner Needs Rigging
3DAI Studio
Freemium
Compare outputs from Meshy, Rodin, and Tripo with the same prompt side by side. Pick the best before committing.
Mid Quality Beginner Comparison Tool

Community Utility Tools
Cats Blender Plugin
Free
One-click optimization for VRChat models. Fix armature, create visemes, merge weights, texture atlas. On life support — successor "Avatar Toolkit" incoming.
Beginner Optimization Life Support
Modular Avatar
Free
Non-destructive prefab assembly in Unity. Drop clothing/accessory prefabs onto your avatar — they merge at build time. Originals untouched.
Intermediate Unity Tool
VRCFury
Free
Non-destructive toggles, clothing attachment, gesture management, controller merging. Makes a copy before upload — originals safe.
Intermediate Unity Tool Don't mix w/ MA
d4rk Optimizer
Free
Auto avatar optimization at build time. Merges meshes, strips unused data, optimizes materials. Works with both MA and VRCFury.
