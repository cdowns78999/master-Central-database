## RESEARCH METHOD PER LABEL

1. **Identify 4 recent artists** — based on known roster + recent release activity
2. **Collect social handles** — Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, Spotify
3. **Estimate metrics** — followers, engagement, trend direction
4. **Record recent release** — song title + approximate date
5. **Calculate aggregates** — total reach, platform score, activity level
