The Vision

Where world-class musicianship meets constitutional AI

Music education has always been humanity's most direct path to cognitive growth, emotional intelligence, and cultural connection. For too long, it's been gatekept by geography, access, and cost. Anthropic's commitment to AI that is safe, honest, and reliable is the exact foundation music learners — and teachers — have been waiting for.

This isn't about replacing human music teachers. It's about giving every student on the planet access to a world-class musical partner that never burns out, never cuts corners, and never stops caring.
