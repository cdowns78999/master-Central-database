Sound Stack

What It Sounds Like

Three sonic references. Not "sounds like X" — sounds like the collision of these three.

Operatic Spine

Real bel-canto technique. Held notes, real vibrato, full lungs. No auto-tune on sustained vowels.

ref: Anna Netrebko · early Renée Fleming
Cinematic Shadow

Strings that sound like soundtrack, not sample. Sparse piano. Sub-bass that moves like an undercurrent.

ref: Jóhann Jóhannsson · FKA twigs MAGDALENE
Masked Pop Edge

Trap skeletons under the orchestration. Hi-hat rolls under a chorale. Contrast is the hook.

ref: Ghostemane (mask) · Sleep Token (anonymity)
Release Calendar · 12 Months

The Drop Plan

Four rungs across the first year. Each release tightens the world and expands the audience. Small and deliberate — no splash-and-fade.

Month 01 · Reveal

Single: "Sigil"

Cold launch. Spotify artist page live. No interviews, no press outreach. One static cover image (the sigil), zero alt-text backstory. Let the music and the mystery do the intro.

Month 04 · Deepen

Single: "Candlewax"

Second track drops. First lyric video (silhouette only). A short, ambiguous press note sent to 3 curated blogs. Playlist pitches through Spotify for Artists.

Month 08 · Expand

EP: "Four Veils"

5-track EP. Physical limited edition (100 copies) pressed on wax-seal-style packaging. Sold only through persona's own site. Feature one contributing producer by first name only.

Month 12 · Mythologize

Short Film + Single: "Vespers"

Six-minute masked film released alongside the single. Anchors the world visually. First live performance announced — one show, masked, no photography allowed.

Keeping the Masks Separate

Persona · Isolation

The Koi (EPK) and the Spotify Alias must never cross. Same voice, different worlds, different ledgers.

  • Different distribution account (DistroKid / TuneCore) under the alias LLC — never shared with Koi.
  • Different streaming artist pages — no "appears on," no cross-links, no shared playlists.
  • Different vocal treatment so spectral analysis isn't an easy match: EQ shelf, pitch-shift 50 cents, different mic chain.
  • Different lyrical subject matter — Koi sings from self, alias sings from character.
  • Different press footprint — Koi does interviews, alias does not.
  • Different payment stream — alias income into persona LLC bank only.
