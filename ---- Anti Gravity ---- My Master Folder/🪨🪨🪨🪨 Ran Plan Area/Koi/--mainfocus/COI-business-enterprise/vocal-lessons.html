Section 02 · The Build Plan

The Ten Days

One concrete task per day. Builds the EPK from scaffold to ship. Tap a day to mark it done — progress is saved locally.

Day 1

Unify the Structure

Merge the multi-page EPK (bio, music, press) into the Prototype's interaction pattern. Export colors, fonts, and timings to a shared tokens.css.

Why · You can't polish what isn't unified.
Day 2

Lock the Hero

Choose the Candlelit Cursor hero. Swap the SVG portrait for a styled 3:4 photo placeholder. Wire the candle-flame cursor.

Why · The hero sells the EPK in 3 seconds.
Day 3

Music Panel

Build the left panel with discography + Wavesurfer.js inline player. Per track: play-in-place, open full-page, Spotify link.

Why · Audio is the heartbeat — get it working early.
Day 4

Bio & Press Panel

Right panel: short bio (60 words) + full bio scroll-reveal with clip-path. Three press quotes as floating anchored asides.

Why · Bio is static, but the choreography matters.
Day 5

Supplementary Pages

Build Music, Dates, Press, and Contact pages with View Transitions between them. Wax-seal contact button that cracks to email.

Why · Content-forward pages; scope is simpler here.
Day 6

Scroll-Driven Motion

Site-wide animation-timeline: view() for fade/blur/translate. Add feTurbulence grain overlay at 12%.

Why · Research becomes real — and it's 60fps.
Day 7

Micro-Interactions

Variable-font hover on display type. Perlin-noise candle flicker. Ornate frame conic-gradient drift (18s loop).

Why · Details cement the vibe — all felt, never announced.
Day 8

Mobile Breakpoint

Under 768px: side zones become top/bottom toggles. Panels slide vertically. Test on 360px.

Why · Half the traffic arrives on phones.
Day 9

Perf + Accessibility

Above-the-fold JS under 200KB. Self-host fonts. prefers-reduced-motion fallback. APCA 7:1 contrast. Skip links + keyboard nav.

Why · Press on hotel wifi will thank you.
Day 10

Deploy + Ship

Netlify with immutable caching. Test View Transitions. Open Graph / Twitter Card meta with crimson-tinted preview. Ship.

Why · Live site beats perfect draft.
