GUARANTEE DEAL

Reference · Key Fact

Why this matters

Guarantee deal: the artist receives a fixed payment regardless of ticket sales. The promoter assumes all financial risk. Common for established acts with proven draw. Typical club-level guarantees range from $200–$5,000 per show for independent artists; mid-level acts command $10,000–$100,000. Building a sustainable touring career follows a clear progression: local shows → regional circuit → national club circuit → festival bookings → headline tours.

Guarantee vs. percentage

GUARANTEE VS PERCENTAGE

Reference · Key Fact

Why this matters

Guarantee vs. percentage: the most common structure for established artists is 'whichever is greater' — a guaranteed floor (say $10,000) plus the right to receive their percentage if ticket sales exceed what the guarantee would cover. This protects the artist from a bad night while preserving upside. Building a sustainable touring career follows a clear progression: local shows → regional circuit → national club circuit → festival bookings → headline tours.
