Publishing 101

The composition is a separate asset from the recording — and the deal you sign on it can outlive you by 70+ years.

  1. Writer's share is sacred Composition royalties always split 50/50 writer/publisher. The writer's half is yours forever and flows directly through your PRO — no publisher can ever touch it.
  2. Admin ≠ Publisher An admin collects for a 10–25% cut and gives the copyright back when the deal ends. A publisher co-owns the copyright — sometimes for the life of it. Wildly different deals.
  3. 3 steps to indie publishing Register with a PRO (ASCAP / BMI). Sign with a publishing administrator (Songtrust / TuneCore Pub). Then register every release with PRO + admin + MLC. No skipped songs.
Next move Confirm your PRO + admin setup is live, then walk every released song through the 3-step registration. If a deal is on the table, read the reversion clause before signing.
