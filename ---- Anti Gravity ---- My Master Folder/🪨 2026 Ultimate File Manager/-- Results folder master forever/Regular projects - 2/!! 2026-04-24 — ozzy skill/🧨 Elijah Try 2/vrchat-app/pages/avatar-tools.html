Modular Avatar
Drag-and-drop avatar assembly on the NDMF framework — menus, armature merges, toggles.

  • Why: Eliminates manual animator merging — just drop components and build

Avatar Optimizer (AAO)
Non-destructive optimization utilities — runs on build, auto-optimizes meshes, materials, bones.
  • What: Part of the NDMF ecosystem — zero-destructive optimization
  • How: Applied on play mode or build — strips unused geometry, merges materials
  • Why: One-click optimization that doesn't destroy your source avatar

Reduces skinned mesh and material count before upload. Non-destructive — creates optimized copy.

  • Why: Safe and reliable — the "create optimized copy" approach protects your work
