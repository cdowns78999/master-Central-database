05

What an artist walks away with

core takeaways
  • Three platforms, three contractsTikTok = discovery (cold audience), Instagram = community (warm fans), YouTube = monetization (long-form funnel). Don't treat them the same.
  • TikTok is the discovery engine84% of Billboard Global 200 entries in 2024 first went viral on TikTok. If you're trying to get found, this is the biggest lever.
  • Watch-time beats follower countAll three platforms prioritize completion rate, watch-time, and engagement velocity over raw follower count. A small, engaged audience outperforms a large, passive one.
  • Cadence is consistency, not volumeTikTok 1–3×/day, IG Reels 3–5×/week, YouTube Shorts 3–5×/week. Predictable schedules win — taking weeks off resets algorithmic momentum.
  • Don't cross-post identical contentTikTok-watermarked Reels get suppressed by Instagram. Each platform needs platform-native edits, captions, and hooks.
  • Engage, don't broadcastPromotional-only posts without comment replies, duets, or stitches see lower reach. Engagement velocity is a ranking signal across all three platforms.
  • Brand coherence beats viralityRandom trending sounds with no brand connection don't convert to followers or streams. Anchor every post to your artist identity.
  • Read your analyticsWatch-time drop-off, hook performance, and audience retention are the most actionable growth signals — don't ignore them.

T

TikTok · Discovery

FYP-driven cold-audience engine

Reference · Platform Contract

Why TikTok owns discovery

TikTok-correlated artists see an average 11% week-over-week streaming growth rate compared to 3% for non-TikTok artists. 84% of songs entering the Billboard Global 200 in 2024 first went viral on TikTok. The FYP is the highest-leverage discovery surface in music.

  • Ranking signals: completion rate, re-watch rate, share rate, sound-save rate
  • Cadence: 1–3× per day, minimum 5× per week
  • Content contract: entertainment + authenticity (not polish)
  • Avoid: re-posting TikTok-watermarked clips to other platforms
IG

Instagram · Community

Warm-fanbase amplifier

Reference · Platform Contract

Why IG owns community

Instagram Reels weighs original audio creation, cross-posting behavior (Reels shared to Stories), and follower engagement history. IG benefits artists who already have an engaged fanbase more than TikTok does for cold discovery.

  • Ranking signals: original audio, cross-posting, follower engagement
  • Cadence: 3–5× per week
  • Content contract: aesthetic + aspiration
  • Amplify: cross-post Reels to Stories for compounding reach
YT

YouTube · Monetize

Long-form funnel

Reference · Platform Contract

Why YouTube owns monetization

YouTube Shorts favors content that drives viewers to long-form video. Artists who use Shorts as a funnel to official videos, documentaries, or live content see the highest subscriber conversion — and long-form is where AdSense, channel memberships, and Super Thanks pay out.

  • Ranking signals: drives views to long-form video, watch-time on the channel
  • Cadence: 3–5× per week
  • Content contract: depth + story
  • Pair every Short with a related long-form upload
