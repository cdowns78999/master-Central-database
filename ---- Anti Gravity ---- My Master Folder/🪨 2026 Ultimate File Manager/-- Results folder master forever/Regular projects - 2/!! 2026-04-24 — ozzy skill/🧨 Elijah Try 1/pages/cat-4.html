SYNC LICENSING REVENUE

Reference · Key Fact

Why this matters

Sync licensing revenue hit $650 million in trade revenue in 2024 (up 7.4% year-over-year). The broader music licensing ecosystem is projected to reach $12.9 billion by 2033. Getting music placed in sync involves three main channels: (1) direct submission to music supervisors (rarely accepted cold — requires relationships or agent representation), (2) licensing through a sync library or agency (most accessible for independents), and (3) direct-to-brand pitching for advertising placements.
