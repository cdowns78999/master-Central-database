Building Your Team

Five roles, distinct functions, hired in a specific order — get the order wrong and the math goes sideways.

  1. Five roles, distinct functions Manager, entertainment attorney, booking agent, publicist, and business manager each serve a distinct function — none is a substitute for another.
  2. Attorney is hire #1 An entertainment attorney comes before any signed contract, including the management deal. $250–600/hour or $500–5,000 flat per transaction is the price of legal cover.
  3. Manager is highest leverage — commission-only aligns it Per Berklee, the manager is the most impactful team member and the one artists most often acquire incorrectly. Standard is 15–20% of gross; monthly retainers before results = misaligned incentives.
Next move Before any new contract crosses your desk, line up an entertainment attorney for a one-hour review — that single hour is the cheapest insurance you'll buy this year.
