Music Publishing

Music publishing is the business of managing, licensing, and monetizing the underlying composition (melody + lyrics) of a song — separate from the sound recording.

Publishing royalties split

PUBLISHING ROYALTIES SPLIT

Publishing royalties split 50/50 between the writer's share (always goes to the songwriter directly via their PRO) and the publisher's share (goes to the publisher, who pays the songwriter per their deal).

Admin publishing deals

ADMIN PUBLISHING DEALS

Admin publishing deals: songwriter retains 100% copyright ownership. Publisher administers collection globally and takes 10–25% commission during the deal term only. The publisher's percentage ends when the deal ends.
