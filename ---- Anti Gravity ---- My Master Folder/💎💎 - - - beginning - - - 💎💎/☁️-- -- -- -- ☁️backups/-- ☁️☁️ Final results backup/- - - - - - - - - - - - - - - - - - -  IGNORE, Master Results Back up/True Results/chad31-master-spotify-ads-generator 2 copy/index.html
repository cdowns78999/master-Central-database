What This Data Actually Means
$

Social Ads Are Burning Money

At 10x more listeners per dollar, every $100 on Marquee delivers what $1,000 on Instagram/TikTok ads would. Even the worst-performing release in Spotify's study still hit 5x. The floor of Marquee beats the ceiling of social. This isn't marginal — this is a completely different tier of ROI. If you're running social ads to drive streams, you're lighting cash on fire.

⇄

The Sequence Multiplier is Real

Marquee alone = 8x lift. Marquee then Showcase = 40x lift. That's not additive, it's multiplicative. The second tool doesn't just add — it compounds the first. This means you should never run Marquee without planning a Showcase follow-up. The budget for both is less than what most teams waste on a single social campaign.

🌎

58% of Discovery is International

More than half of the listeners Discovery Mode brings you have never heard you before — and they're overseas. This means a US-based artist with no international marketing can still build a global fanbase at zero upfront cost. Combined with Showcase's 30+ market targeting, you can build touring demand in cities before you've ever played there.

📈

The 2% That Drives 50%

Jillian Rossi's data proved that monthly active listeners (just 2% of total audience) drove over 50% of her streams. This means the game isn't about total follower count — it's about activating the right listeners at the right time. Marquee's 24-38% save rate means a third of people who see it are converting to that high-value 2%.

⚠

The Lapsed Listener Goldmine

Magic City Hippies reactivated 25% of their lapsed listeners with a single Marquee. These are people who already liked the artist enough to stream before — they just forgot. One $100 campaign brought a quarter of them back. That's not customer acquisition, that's re-engagement at near-zero friction. Previously Active is the highest-leverage audience segment in the entire Campaign Kit — they already have intent, they just need a nudge.
