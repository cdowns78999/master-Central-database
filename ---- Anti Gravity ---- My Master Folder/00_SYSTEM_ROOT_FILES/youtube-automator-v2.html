YouTube Automator

How it works

Welcome to the YouTube Automator. This board helps you visualize and track your content production pipeline.

  • Phases: Add sequential stages (e.g., Ideas, Research, Production).
  • Boxes: Each phase starts with 5 boxes. Click [+] to add up to 10.
  • Dynamic Flow: Use the [+ Add Phase] button under any phase to chain the next step.
  • Persistence: Your progress is tracked in your browser's local storage. You can also export the board to a JSON file.
