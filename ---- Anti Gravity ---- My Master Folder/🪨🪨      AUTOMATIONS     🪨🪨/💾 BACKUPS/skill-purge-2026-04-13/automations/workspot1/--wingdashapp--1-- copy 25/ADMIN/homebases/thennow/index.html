THENNOW Announces New Music Campaign with Ahead Artist Solutions
Los Angeles, CA — March 25, 2026 12:41 PM —
THENNOW has partnered with Ahead Artist Solutions to launch a comprehensive music promotion campaign spanning multiple platforms and media outlets. The campaign aims to increase visibility, grow audience engagement, and establish a stronger presence across key digital channels.
The multi-phase approach includes targeted streaming campaigns, premium press coverage in leading music publications, and strategic social media amplification. Each component has been carefully designed to complement the others, creating a unified narrative that resonates with both existing fans and new audiences.
"We believe in building sustainable growth for our artists," said the Ahead Artist Solutions team. "This campaign represents a holistic approach to music marketing that prioritizes authenticity and long-term results."
