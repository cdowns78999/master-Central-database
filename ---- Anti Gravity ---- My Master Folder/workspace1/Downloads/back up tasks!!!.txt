The core of the system is this:

You simply select what 'audience personality type' you'd like to 'link,' per song.

The options available to advertise toward are:


Super listeners – 15+ intentional streams last 28 days.
Moderate listeners – 3–14 intentional streams last 28 days.
Light listeners – 1–2 intentional streams last 28 days.
Previously active listeners – no intentional streams last 28 days, but listened before.
Programmed listeners – only heard via programmed sources, no active listening in 2+ years.
Potential listeners – haven’t listened in 2+ years (or ever), but likely to enjoy your music.
​
