17. Emergency Preparedness

MAJOR RESTRUCTURE

Family Planning (Req 2-4)

No longer strictly "first five situations". Expanded to 21 situations (gas leaks, blizzards, wildland fires). Shift to personal kit preparation and home safe inspections.

Rescue Signaling (Req 6)

Added aircraft or drones as rescue targets. Signaling for air, ground, and water.

19. Environmental Science

Field Observations (Req 2)

Complete shift from "Conduct experiments" to multi-day field observation (sight, sound, smell) of ecosystems.

Pollution & Acts (Req 3-5)

Deep dive into Clean Air Act, Clean Water Act, and Superfund sites. Significant policy focus added.
