2025 Updates: Batch 1

Detailed requirement diffs for Merit Badges 1-10. Effective January 1, 2025.

1. Animal Science

MINOR CHANGE
Requirement 3 Update

Updated to include modern sustainable ranching practices and digital health monitoring. Research three methods of electronic identification used in livestock.

5. Chemistry

MAJOR SHIFT
Core Philosophy

Requirement focus has shifted from theoretical molecular bonds to practical laboratory safety, SDS (Safety Data Sheets), and environmental impact analysis of chemical waste.

8. Cooking

SIGNIFICANT UPDATE
Safety & Allergies

Enhanced emphasis on food allergy safety protocols and cross-contamination prevention. New requirement for "Outdoor Code" integration in camp cooking.

Counselor Action Required

Troop 553B Scouts starting these badges after Jan 1, 2025 must adhere to these revised standards. Please ensure your Blue Card reflects the 2025 requirement set.
