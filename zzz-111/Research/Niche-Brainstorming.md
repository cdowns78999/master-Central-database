# YouTube Niche Brainstorming Guide

To find the perfect niche, let's look at the intersection of your **Passions**, **Skills**, and **Market Demand**.

## 1. Passions (What do you love?)
- What topics could you talk about for 30 minutes with no preparation?
- What do you do in your free time?
- What kind of YouTube videos do you enjoy watching?

## 2. Skills (What are you good at?)
- What do people often ask you for help with?
- Do you have any professional expertise or unique hobbies?
- Are you good at teaching, entertaining, or storytelling?

## 3. Market Demand (What do people want?)
- Are there popular keywords related to your interests?
- Can you offer a unique perspective that's missing from current creators?

---

### Potential Niches to Consider:
- **Tech & Productivity:** Reviewing tools, setup tours, software tutorials.
- **Gaming:** Let's Plays, walkthroughs, gaming news.
- **Lifestyle & Vlogging:** Daily routines, travel, personal development.
- **Education/Tutorials:** Teaching a specific skill (coding, cooking, DIY).
- **Commentary/Essays:** Deep dives into pop culture, news, or trends.
