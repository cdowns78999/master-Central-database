## Stages Definition
- **Idea:** Initial concept brainstorming.
- **Scripting:** Writing the outline or full script.
- **Filming:** Capturing the footage.
- **Editing:** Post-production (cuts, b-roll, audio).
- **Published:** Live on YouTube!
