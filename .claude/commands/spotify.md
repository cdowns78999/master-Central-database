## THE 4 SPOTIFY CAMPAIGN KIT TOOLS

### 1. Playlist Pitching (FREE)
- Submit unreleased tracks to Spotify's editorial team
- Must pitch at least 7 days before release
- 74% of editorial placements go to artists with < 100K monthly listeners
- A single editorial placement can generate 100K+ streams
- Source: "How to Meet Your Music Goals — Jay Wheeler"

### 2. Discovery Mode (FREE — no upfront cost)
- Prioritizes tracks in algorithmic recommendations (Radio, Autoplay)
- Cost: commission on streams from boosted listens only
- 31% of Discovery Mode listeners are brand-new to the artist
- +50% saves, +44% playlist adds, +37% follows for Discovery-boosted tracks
- 385-440% stream lift documented across case studies
- Sources: "How Magic City Hippies Sequenced Marquee, Canvas & Discovery Mode", "How to Meet Your Music Goals — Discovery Mode", "Seven Success Stories — William Black / Khalia"

### 3. Showcase ($100 minimum, CPC ~$0.40)
- Banner card on Spotify Home feed
- **Eligibility**: 1,000 streams in 28 days in a target market
- 7 headline types: New music, Recently released, On tour, Release anniversary, Getting buzz, Seasonal vibes, Releasing music soon
- Reached listeners are 6x more likely to stream
- >10% of Showcase listeners saved the music
- ~20% explored more of the artist's catalog
- 1 in 4 listeners saved or playlisted from Showcase
- Sources: "Getting Started with Showcase — How It Works", "How to Meet Your Music Goals — Goth Babe", "Seven Success Stories — Money Man", "Seven Success Stories — William Black"

### 4. Marquee ($100 minimum)
- Full-screen recommendation on app open
- **Eligibility**: 5,000 monthly active listeners (MAL)
- Marquee alone = 8x lift in new active listeners
- **Marquee + Showcase combined = 40x lift in new active listeners** (5x more than Marquee alone)
- This 40x compound is true regardless of spend level
- Sources: "Seven Success Stories — Opening Research", "Getting Started with Showcase — Catalog Streams"

## THE 6 SPOTIFY AUDIENCE SEGMENTS

### Active Listeners
1. **Super Listeners** — Intentional streamers in the last 28 days (highest intent)
2. **Moderate Listeners** — Regular but less frequent streamers in the last 28 days
3. **Light Listeners** — Occasional listeners in the last 28 days

### Inactive / New Listeners
4. **Previously Active** — Used to listen, haven't in 28+ days (reactivation targets)
5. **Programmed** — Heard the artist only via playlists/radio, never sought them out (first direct contact targets)
6. **Potential** — Never heard the artist but match the listener profile (pure discovery targets)
